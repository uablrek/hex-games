|Welcome0|
Welcome to a browser version of the Avalon Hill board game
"Wooden Ships & Iron Men".

|Welcome|
^The map is draggable, but not scrollable.
You can click on any ship to bring up it's stats.
^
^Enter - Start the game
^Esc - Un-select ship
^i - Toggle hex id's
^p - Save the map as png
|zoom|
^NOTE: If you reload or close the page, your progress is lost!
If you want to adjust the zoom-level in your browser, do that now,
and then reload.

|Test Mode|

Test-Mode is active. AI is disabled, but you may query the AI for
movement. If the "Flying Duchman" is in play, it has movement 7 in all
directions. No damage is taken from gun-fire.
^
^Extra keys in Planning phase:
^
^j - Rotate wind direction
^n - Display Hex-Index
^m - Get movement from AI

|Planning|
Select a ship by clicking it, then add movement steps.
^
^Enter,q - Proceed
^l - Let the AI play for you (Lazy)
^c,Backspace - Clear Movement
^Space - Next unmoved Ship
^= - Copy to all friendly ships
^arrowL,a - Turn Left
^arrowR,d - Turn Right
^arrowUp,w - Forward
^arrowDn,s - Backing sails
^b - Battle Sails
^f - Full sails
^
^Invalid steps are simply ignored.
^Changing sails will be done after combat.

|Movement|
All vessels move. Collisions may occur.

|Combat|
Ships fires broadsides. Click on a friendly ship to mark possible targets.
^
^Enter,q - Proceed
^l - Let the AI play for you (Lazy)
^f - Toggle field-of-fire
^h - Fire guns at hull (range <= 5)
^r - Fire guns at rigging
^Space - Select target (if many)
