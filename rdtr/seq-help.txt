|game/Connect to server|
Select "Connect" for multi-player, or "Local" to play both sides.
The first to connect becomes "Axis", and the second "Allies"

|turn/Declaration of War (DoW)|
Players announce Declaration of War on major (35brp) or minor (10brp)
countries.

|turn/Announce front action|
Players announce which of: Offensive (15brp), Attrition or Pass action,
for all fronts.

|turn/Movement|
All ground units may move, air units may stage, and naval unit may
change base. In Pass option units may only move within controlled
territory. Naval units providing supply are marked.

|offensive/Attacker naval+air|
14.11 Attacker announces all naval and air missions. All Counterair
missions (28.43) are resolved.

|offensive/Defender inteception+DAS|
14.12 Defender announces any allocations of Defensive Air Support,
any air attacks on naval forces at sea, and any naval Interception
attempts.

|offensive/Sea Transport Missions|
14.14 Successful Sea Transport missions (29.42) are concluded and the
naval units returned to their base.

|offensive/Airborne drops|
14.15 Attacker may make airborne drops.

|offensive/Combat|
Resolve combat in any order. Move exploiting units to breakthrough
hexes.

|offensive/Exploitation|
Exploiting units move, and have a combat phase
