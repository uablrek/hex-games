|game/Initial Deplyment|
Welcome to the Napoleon at Waterloo game. The French starts first. Hit
'Enter' to move to the next phase. Hit 'h' for more keyboard help.

|player/Reinforcements|
Reinforcements are available

|player/Movement|
Click on a friendly unit to mark all possible moves. Click on a marker
to move the unit there. Moved units have a red mark. Regret a move by
hitting 'r'. Unit maybe temporary stacked during movement, hit 'Space'
to rotate the stack.

|player/Combat|
Click on an enemy unit to select it as a target for attack. It will be
marked with a cross-hair. Select friendly units to attack the
target. Check the odds at the CRT. When ready, hit 'a' to commence the
attack.
^^NOTE: All marked unit must attack or be attacked! The game can
*not* check this during the combat phase, but friendly units adjacent
to a non-attacked enemy unit will be removed after the combat phase!!

|game/Declare Winner|
The current player is the winner. If the current player is 'None' the
game is a tie

|overstacked|
There are overstacked counters. You can either remove units, or
regret/redo moves until the stacking rules are obeyed.

|kbd-help|
Keyboard help:
^Enter - Next phase
^Esc - Clear markers
^Space - Rotate stack
^a - Attack
^r - Regret move
^d - Delete unit
